WELCOME TO EDGECRAFT

#BringHappinessInside

EdgeCraft Pvt. Ltd. (Formerly known as JM Lifestyle Interior Projects Pvt. Ltd.) is the largest and most reliable home interior design company in Navi Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, Bangalore, Kerala,Mysore, Chennai, Nagercoil, Coimbatore, Madurai & Mangalore with more than 1400+ employees. It is one of the prominent names in 100% customized furniture and modular kitchens. Here, the manufacturer deals directly with clients, avoiding all intermediaries and thus reducing costs. Durability of products, systematic procedure for work and teamwork of highly efficient people make this firm the best in the industry. Contemporary designs and creative space plans by professionals ensure elegant home furnishing.

This way, the company is able to meet the expectations of clients since its inception in the year 2004.

At EdgeCraft We believe furniture should be as unique as the people who use it. That's why we specialize in custom handcrafted furniture that brings your vision to life—the finest detail. Whether you're designing a bold statement piece or tailoring your space with functionality in mind, we're here to build furniture that fits your style, your space, and your story.

Founded on a passion for craftsmanship and innovation, EdgeCraft blends traditional techniques with modern design to create furniture that stands out for its quality and character. Every piece is made-to-order, using premium materials and sustainable practices, ensuring durability and timeless appeal. Our team of skilled artisans and designers works closely with each client, offering personalized solutions for homes, offices, and commercial spaces—from selecting finishes to adjusting dimensions.
